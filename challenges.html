year of challenges 2018

A key value of mine is personal growth - it is my motivator, driving me to challenge myself mentally and physically, and when I achieve success it is my self-fulfilment. I'm not perfect, but I'm always striving to do better than I did before. Whenever I do psychometric tests, it always shows that one of my strengths is competitiveness and ambition - which are perhaps symptoms of this internal drive.

For that reason, I decided to call it out this year and set myself actual challenges, to push myself to even higher levels of performance. I've listed below the ones I'm working on, along with the ways that I can keep myself accountable. I am already grateful for the support that friends and family have offered, as that too is very motivating (particularly when the initial excitement about undertaking a challenge has worn off!) Please feel free to follow / add / like or comment on my activities.

  • Running the London Winter Run 10K challenge on 4th February, for Cancer Research UK (please sponsor me!) You can follow my runs on Strava.
  • Learning to speak German, starting with daily practice on Duolingo
  • Studying courses equivalent to a MBA - I might even blog about it yet!
